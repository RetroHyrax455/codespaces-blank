Information, Facts, and Uses of Fruits

Fruits are nature's candy, grown straight from the earthy soil some sprout from trees some sprout from bushes. They come in a variety of different colors, shapes, and sizes as well as ranging in many different tastes from sweet, to sour, and bitter.

Fruits can be fleshy or dry, fleshy fruits are juicy and soft. For example, blueberry, strawberries, and razzberries. While dry fruits consist of raizins, almonds, walnuts

Fruits are good for you, they contain nutritions like vitamins, minerals, and dietary fibers.

Fruts are commonly eaten plainly, though, there are many ways fruits can be used such as being used in smoothies, milkshakes, baking, jellies, desserts, and savory dishes.
